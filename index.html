Team Rankings

How We Calculate Team Scores

Our Composite Score (0-100) ranks teams for futures betting value by weighting key efficiency metrics:

40% Net Rating
20% Offensive Rating
20% Defensive Rating
10% KenPom Rank
10% Conference Strength

NET (Net Rating) = Offensive Rating Defensive Rating. It measures points scored vs allowed per 100 possessions, adjusted for opponent strength. A +15 NET is elite; negative means below average.

March Madness

Tournament Contender Analysis

Based on 25 years of March Madness data, here are the key metrics that predict tournament success:

Champion Profile
  • Top 20 in BOTH offense & defense efficiency
  • 1, 2, or 3 seed (33 of 34 champs since 1985)
  • Top 6 overall in advanced metrics
  • Power conference team
Final Four Profile
  • Top 25 in offensive efficiency
  • Top 25 in defensive efficiency
  • 1-4 seed (37 of 40 FF teams last decade)
  • Top 25 in margin of victory
Elite 8 Profile
  • Top 40 in offensive efficiency
  • Top 40 in defensive efficiency
  • Usually 1-5 seed
  • Balanced on both ends
